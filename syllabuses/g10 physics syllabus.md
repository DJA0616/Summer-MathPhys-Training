# G10 Physics Syllabus (SY 2025-2026)

## First Quarter

- **Introduction to Waves**
  - Wave Intensity
  - Wave Speed
  - Wave Equation
  - Wave Properties
  - Superposition of Waves
- **Introduction to Simple Harmonic Motion**
  - Motion
  - Energy and Simple Harmonic Motion
  - Sinusoidal Nature of Simple Harmonic Motion
  - Period of a Simple Pendulum
  - Sound as a Wave
  - Wave and Musical Properties
  - Sound Reflection
  - Sound Refraction and Diffraction
  - Doppler Effect (optional topic)

*Long Exam 1: August 14/15, 2025*  
*Long Exam 2: September 22-24, 2025*

-----

## Second Quarter

- **Light as an Electromagnetic Wave**
  - Law of Reflection
  - Snell’s Law of Refraction
  - Total Internal Reflection
  - Apparent Depth
  - Introduction to Mirrors
  - Concave and Convex Mirrors
- **Mirror Equation**
  - Introduction to Lenses
  - Image Formation by Converging Lenses
  - Image Formation by Diverging Lenses
  - Concave and Convex Lenses
  - Thin Lens Equation

*Long Exam 1: October 23/24, 2025*  
*Long Exam 2: December 2-4, 2025*

-----

## Third Quarter

- **Electrostatic Charges and Charge Transfer**
  - Coulomb’s Law
  - Electric Field
  - Vector Summation of Electric Fields
- **Electric Potential and Potential Energy**
  - Applications of Electrostatics
  - Current
  - Resistivity and Resistance
  - Ohm’s Law
  - Resistors and Resistor Combinations
  - Circuits

*Long Exam 1: January 29/30, 2025*

-----

## Fourth Quarter

- **Magnetic Materials**
  - Magnetic Field Lines
  - Magnetic Field of Current
  - Configurations
  - Magnetic Force on a Moving Charge
  - Magnetic Force on Current
- **Motion of a Charge in a Magnetic Field**
  - Faraday’s Law
  - Lenz’s Law

*Long Exam 1: March 19/20, 2025*  
*Long Exam 2: April 27-29, 2025*
